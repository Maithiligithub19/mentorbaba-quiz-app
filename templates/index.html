QuizXMentor is a comprehensive web-based quiz application with advanced timer functionality and auto-progression features. Built with modern web technologies, it offers a seamless timed quiz experience with Excel-based question management for educators and students.

How QuizXMentor Works

QuizXMentor follows a simple yet powerful workflow designed for maximum efficiency and user experience:

1

Account Setup

Create your secure account with email registration and login to access your personalized dashboard with quiz history and progress tracking.

2

Question Upload & Confirmation

Upload your quiz questions using our Excel template format with multiple choice options (A-D) and correct answers. Review quiz details and instructions before starting.

3

Timed Quiz Experience

Take quiz with 30-second timer per question, automatic progression, progress tracking, and intuitive navigation between questions with real-time feedback.

4

Detailed Results

Receive comprehensive results with scores, percentages, and detailed analytics. Track your performance and identify areas for improvement.
